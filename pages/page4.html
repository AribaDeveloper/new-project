Rose

Roses are beautiful flowers loved by many.they come in various colors like red,pink,white and yellow. Roses have delicate petals and a sweet fragrance.They grow on bushes and require sunlight and water to thrive. Roses are used in bouquets,decorations,and perfumes.They are also used in many home remedies and self care products.It is used in several recipes like rose milk or rose lassi, or even rose flavoured candy,chocolates and sweets.Roses stand out among all other beautiful and colorful flowers.The rose is one of the most well-known flowers in the world.It is one of the most beautiful flowers.Even at the bud stage, it looks extremely pretty like a ruby gemstone,when it blooms, the rose has many layers of petals.It has a centre with stamen and pollen like all flowers.A rose bush typically has plenty of thorns to protect the flowers from animals.Although the red rose is the most commonly found colour.

Jasmine

Jasmine is considered a shrub,most closely relative to the olive famiy.There are around 200 species scattered around warm or tropical regions of the globe.Jasmine originates from far east,namely india and china.Jasmine are yellow and white in colour jasmine can either have leaves that fall in autumn.In foods, jasmine is used to flavor beverages,frozen,daily desserts,candy,baked goods,gelatins,and puddings.Jasmine has been used for liver disease to treat hepatitis,liver pain.Jasmine oil contains active ingredients that naturally fight harmful bacteria and viruses within the body and on the skin,helping to prevent sickness, irritation,fungus, and viral infections.It is widely cultivated for it's shinning leaves and clusters of flowers that bloom in summer.

Tulip

Tulips are plants that bloom in early spring.The flowers are cup-shaped and very colorful. They are among the most popular garden flowers.there are about 4000 varieties of tulip.They belong to the lily family.Tulips first grew in south-central asia.People brought the flowers to Europe in the 1500s and later to many diffrent parts of the world.Tulip normally grow from bulbs that are planted autumn each bulb produce a plant each spring for a few years.A tulip plant has two or three thick,bluish green leaves.These are attached at the bottom of the stem.In most types of tulip each stem grows a single flower. Tulip flowers occur in almost every color white,yellow,pink,red,orange,purple,and even brown and black.The color is either solid or streaked.Streaked tulips get their streaks because of a harmless virus.The virus makes the top color disappear in some places.The flowers underlying white or yellow color then shows through.
