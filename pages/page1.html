Chocolate

chocolate, food product made from cocoa beans, consumed as candy and used to make beverages and to flavour or coat various confections and bakery products. Rich in carbohydrates, it has several health benefits and is an excellent source of quick energy.Nowadays, chocolates are considered to be a such a sweet treat, however, four thousand years ago, it was actually an invigorating drink, as well as a mood enhancer.If you love chocolates as a treat or a candy, it is best have some dark chocolates. That is because it is the healthiest type of chocolate as a treat. It has a high content of cocoa butter and it has less or no milk at all. It also contain a lot of antioxidants, like polyphenols. Jelly

jelly, a semitransparent confection consisting of the strained juice of various fruits or vegetables, singly or in combination, sweetened, boiled, slowly simmered, and congealed, often with the aid of pectin, gelatin, or a similar substance.The stiff, chewy consistency of the popular gumdrop and jelly bean candies is imparted by various grain starches. Jellies made from the seaweed extract agar, valued for their clarity and body, are used to coat various candy centres or to make colourful simulated fruit slices.
